QUALITY POLICY

At Tower Roofing, Quality is more than just a word!


It's a way of life!


Starting from the raw material used by Aluminium Rolling Mills(see About Us), which is a ISO 9001:2008 company, the entire process of melting,continous casting,cold rollings of coils, sizing and embossing is under strict quality control.


Even at KolorKote Nigeria Limite-also a ISO 9001:2008 company- the entire process of feedstock selection,pretreatment,coating,oven baking and embossing operations are under strict stage wise quality control. Some of the tests carried out by Kolorkote for Oven Baked Color coated coils are: colour matching as per RAL standards,Guage,Impact test, MEK Rub test,Bend test,Adhesion test etc.


Appropiate Statistical QC pratices are followed to monitor continuous improvement programs on product quality all with ultimate objective of delivering consistently the very best to all our customers in the roofing market.
